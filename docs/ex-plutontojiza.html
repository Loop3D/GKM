Description medium- to coarse-grained granite consists of quartz, K-feldspar, plagioclase and biotite. Apatite, ilmenite, zircon, muscovite and, locally, garnet are accessory minerals; contains rounded grains of quartz visible to the naked eye. Most samples are equigranular
