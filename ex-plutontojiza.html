latojizafinegrainedni

Class(es)
gsgu:Igneous_Facies
URI https://w3id.org/gso/ex-plutontojiza#latojizafinegrained
Description The amount of muscovite and garnet increases, and grain size decreases, towards the eastern and southern pluton borders. contain rounded grains of quartz visible to the naked eye. Most samples are equigranular. Phases are not mapped separately

latojizamainni

Class(es)
gsgu:Igneous_Facies
URI https://w3id.org/gso/ex-plutontojiza#latojizamain
Description medium- to coarse-grained granite consists of quartz, K-feldspar, plagioclase and biotite. Apatite, ilmenite, zircon, muscovite and, locally, garnet are accessory minerals; contains rounded grains of quartz visible to the naked eye. Most samples are equigranular

latojizaporphyriticni

Class(es)
gsgu:Igneous_Facies
URI https://w3id.org/gso/ex-plutontojiza#latojizaporphyritic
Description Some outcrops show porphyritic textures with euhedral, 1–5 cm, K-feldspar phenocrysts within a medium- to coarse-grained matrix; contains rounded grains of quartz visible to the naked eye
